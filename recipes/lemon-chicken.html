Lemon Chicken

Description

Our easy lemon chicken recipe shines with a sunny, lemony zing. Garlic and herbs—plus a glug of white wine—mean that these juicy, tender chicken breasts are as delicious as they are healthy.

Ingredients

  • Lemon in three ways: lemon zest, lemon juice, lemon slices
  • Garlic: fresh garlic cloves and garlic powder
  • Chicken
  • White wine
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degree F.
  2. Pat the chicken breasts dry and place them in a 9 x 13 baking dish.
  3. Mix the olive oil, oregano, thyme, garlic powder, 1 teaspoon salt and pepper to create a thick marinade/paste. Coat the chicken breast with seasoning paste.
  4. Mix together the white wine, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, brown sugar and remaining 1 teaspoon salt. Pour over chicken breast.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes, Baste the chicken with pan juice and bake for another 15 minutes until chicken are 165 degree F
