Mac and Cheese

Description

What’s easier—and way better—than instant mac and cheese? Rich, creamy, homemade Instant Pot mac and cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni for 8 minutes
  2. Melt butter in a pan. Add flour, salt, and pepper. Stir til smooth for 5 minutes
  3. Add cheese to pan, stir for 2 to 4 minutes
  4. Combine macaroni and sauce
